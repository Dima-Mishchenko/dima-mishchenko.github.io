Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I restore my Premium subscription?

  • Open the App
  • Go to Settings or Premium
  • Tap "Restore Purchases"
  • Make sure you're signed in with the Apple ID used for the purchase

2. My data is not syncing or saving

Please try the following:

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Keep the app updated to the latest version
  • Restart the app and try again
  • Make sure background app refresh is enabled

If the issue persists, contact support.

3. Calorie or nutrition values don't seem accurate

Calorie tracking accuracy may be affected by:

  • Incorrect or incomplete food entries
  • Portion size estimates
  • Differences between food brands or preparation methods
  • User-entered data

For best results, double-check quantities and food selections.

4. The app crashes or freezes

  • Restart the app
  • Update to the latest app version
  • Restart your device

If the issue continues, please email us with:

  • Your device model
  • iOS version
  • App version
