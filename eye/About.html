Everything you need to know about Iris AI and your eye privacy.

Iris AI uses the high-resolution camera of your smartphone to capture detail-rich photos of your eye. Computer vision neural networks process the image to isolate the iris stroma, mapping color frequencies, structural crypt spacing, pupil response boundaries, and melanin levels to cross-reference with international eye trait statistics databases.

Absolutely. We take privacy extremely seriously. The biometric extraction processes run entirely locally on-device. Your photo scans are processed without leaving your device. We do not sell or store personal identity-linked biometric indices.

Iris archetypes are categorizations based on structural features. "Jewel" irises feature prominent pigment spot freckles. "Shaker" irises display visible concentric contraction furrows. "Stream" irises display smooth radial fibers extending like rivers. These categories are inspired by behavioral biometrics research linking physiological developments to nervous system patterns.

The scanner extracts thousands of color samples across the iris to calculate a complete RGB/Hex spectrum and map precise percentages of primary and secondary colors. However, physical camera filters, glare, and environmental lighting can shift values slightly. For the most accurate result, take eye photos under natural indirect daylight.

Important Medical Disclaimer: Iris AI is designed solely for entertainment, educational, and general self-discovery purposes. It does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any optical or medical conditions. It does not replace professional eye clinic examinations or medical-grade ophthalmic scans. Always consult an optometrist or medical professional for clinical advice.
