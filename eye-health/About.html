About Eye Lens

Eye Lens is designed for anyone who cares about their vision—from those curious about their unique eye characteristics to individuals actively monitoring their eye wellness.

Our app combines cutting-edge AI technology with science-backed methodologies to provide you with comprehensive insights into your eye health. We believe that understanding your eyes is the first step toward protecting and maintaining your vision for life.

Important: This app is for educational and wellness purposes. It does not diagnose, treat, or prevent any medical conditions. Always consult with a qualified eye care professional for medical advice and comprehensive eye examinations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Eye Lens a medical diagnostic tool?

No, Eye Lens is designed for educational and wellness purposes only. It does not replace professional eye examinations or diagnose medical conditions. Always consult with an eye care professional for medical advice.

How accurate is the eye color analysis?

Our AI-powered analysis uses advanced computer vision algorithms to detect and categorize eye colors with high precision. However, lighting conditions and image quality can affect results.

Is my eye scan data secure?

Yes, all your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information or eye scans with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I use Eye Lens without a premium subscription?

Yes! Eye Lens offers a free version with basic eye scanning and limited vision tests. Premium features unlock advanced analysis, unlimited tests, and comprehensive tracking.

How often should I use the vision tests?

We recommend taking vision tests periodically to track changes over time. However, Eye Lens does not replace regular professional eye examinations recommended by your eye care provider.
