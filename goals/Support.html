Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Premium subscription?

If you reinstalled Goals App or upgraded devices, restoring your purchase is simple:

  • Open Goals App on your iOS device.
  • Go to Settings (or tap on the Premium banner).
  • Tap on the "Restore Purchases" option.
  • Ensure you are logged into the device using the same Apple ID used to subscribe.

How do I link workout logs to Apple Health?

Goals App syncs seamlessly with Apple Health so you don't lose exercise logs:

  • When opening the app for the first time, request HealthKit permissions when prompted.
  • Alternatively, open iOS Settings app > Health > Data Access & Devices > Goals.
  • Ensure permissions for Workouts, Steps, Active Calories, and Weight are turned ON.
  • Logged workouts will write automatically, and your dashboard steps/calorie burns will keep updated in real-time.

Can I duplicate existing workout logs to save time?

Yes! Under the **Exercises** tab, you can create workout templates. To duplicate any template or copy it, tap and hold on the template row and select **Duplicate** to make a direct clone that you can customize. On the main details sheet of a logged workout, tap **Duplicate Workout** to copy the log forward.

How does the Fasting Timer work?

Choose an intermittent fasting protocol (like 16:8) and start the timer when you begin your fast. The companion app will keep tracking the remaining time, display metabolic progress, and log full completions directly into your health dashboard statistics once dismissed.

Troubleshooting Issues

App freezes, crashes, or fails to save logs

If the database fails to write or the UI halts, please try the following steps:

  • Force-quit Goals App and relaunch it.
  • Check if your device has enough free local storage.
  • Ensure you are running the latest app version by checking the App Store.
  • Restart your iOS device.
