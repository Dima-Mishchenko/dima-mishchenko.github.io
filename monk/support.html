Frequently Asked Questions

How does Monk block applications?

Monk integrates locally with Apple's native Screen Time API (FamilyControls) on iOS. When you enable shielding, selected distracting applications are blocked instantly. These blocks remain in place until you complete a mindful ritual check-in.

Do my journals or reflection texts leave my phone?

No. Monk is built as a local-first application. All reflections, check-in journals, stats, and block lists are stored securely in your device's local database and are never uploaded to any external servers.

How do I cancel my Monk premium subscription?

All subscriptions are billed and managed directly through Apple. You can manage or cancel your subscription by opening the App Store app, tapping your profile picture in the top right corner, selecting "Subscriptions," and choosing Monk.

Why am I not redirected back to the Monk app after unlocking?

Please make sure that notifications are enabled for Monk in iOS System Settings. The app uses local notifications to deliver quick link options to return directly to your workspace.
